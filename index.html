Adeegyada Wasaaradda Caafimaadka

In dhammaan carruurta Puntland hesho tallaalada kala duwan ee ka hortagga xanuunada ku dhaca ilmaha

In Wasaaraddu dhisto xarrumo caafimaad oo ku fillan bulshada Puntland isla markaan dayactir ku samayso xarrumaha caafimaadka.

In dadweynaha Puntland la siiyo wacyigelin gelin ku aadan xanuunada kala duwan ee ka jira geyiga iyo ka hortaggooda si loo helo bulsho caafimaad qabta.

In bulshadu hesho dawooyin tayo fiican leh isla markaana la hubiyey si bulshada caafimaadkooda loo Daryeelo.

Nagu Saabsan

Wasaarada Caafimaadka Puntland Waxay Ka shaqaysaa sidii shacabka puntland ay u heli lahaayeen caafimaad heer sareeya, dawooyin tayo fiican leh, isbitaalo casriya, la dagaalanka iyo ka hortagga cudurrada faafa. Wasaaraddu waxay saddex billood ee ugu danbeeyey ay aad ugu hawlanayd la dagaalanka cudurka safmarka ah ee COVID-19 kaas oo haddeer dadka reer Puntland ee qaaday ay gaadhayaan ilaa 190 qof.
