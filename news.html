Nagu Saabsan

Wasaarada Caafimaadka Puntland Waxay Ka shaqaysaa sidii shacabka puntland ay u heli lahaayeen caafimaad heer sareeya, dawooyin tayo fiican leh, isbitaalo casriya, la dagaalanka iyo ka hortagga cudurrada faafa. Wasaaraddu waxay saddex billood ee ugu danbeeyey ay aad ugu hawlanayd la dagaalanka cudurka safmarka ah ee COVID-19 kaas oo haddeer dadka reer Puntland ee qaaday ay gaadhayaan ilaa 190 qof.
